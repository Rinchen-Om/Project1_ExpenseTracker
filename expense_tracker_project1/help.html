Frequently Asked Questions

What is Expense Tracker?
A: An expense tracker is a tool that helps users record and monitor their spending habits, ensuring better financial management.

Why should I use an expense tracker?
A: It helps you stay within budget, identify unnecessary expenses, and plan for future financial goals.

How do I add an expense?
A: Navigate to the Expense page, enter details like category, amount, and date, then submit.

Can I track my income and balance?
A: Yes! Most expense trackers allow users to monitor income, expenses, and overall balance.
